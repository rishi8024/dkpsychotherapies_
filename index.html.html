About

With over 20 years of experience in mental health care, I have worked across acute mental health services, urgent care, secondary care, and primary care within the NHS. Alongside this, I bring 5 years of experience in private practice as a psychotherapist. I am a BABCP-accredited Cognitive Behavioural Therapist (CBT) and an EMDR Europe-accredited EMDR practitioner. In addition to clinical practice, I have contributed to the training and development of future therapists as a clinical supervisor at the University of Birmingham.

Our Specializations

Therapies & Approaches

Core Therapies

CBT & EMDR

I am experienced in delivering evidence-based Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) and Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing (EMDR) for a wide range of difficulties.

Integrative Approaches

Counselling & More

I also provide therapy using integrative approaches including counselling, solution-focused therapy, Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT), mindfulness, and compassion-focused therapy.

Inclusive Practice

Languages & Communities

I offer sessions in English, Hindi, Punjabi, and Urdu, and bring experience working with diverse communities, cultures, and sexual orientations, ensuring therapy is inclusive and respectful.
